# Noah-Bridge DAO Governance

## 1. Membership & Roles
- **Founder (Noah):** The visionary and architect of the bridge.
- **Node Partners (Bridge Builders):** Individuals hosting the `index.php` bridge on their servers.
- **Contributors:** Developers and testers optimizing the light-weight engine.

## 2. Decision Making (Snapshot)
All major project directions will be decided through **Snapshot** voting.
- **Voting Power:** 1 Partner = 1 Vote (Verified by bridge deployment).
- **Proposals:** Any partner can propose improvements or new legacy device support.

## 3. Technical Standards
- **Open Source:** All bridge code must remain under the **GPL-3.0** license.
- **Lightweight First:** Every update must prioritize loading speed on 3G/legacy browsers.
- **Security:** Partners are responsible for securing their own API keys.

## 4. How to Join
1. Fork this repository.
2. Deploy the bridge to your server.
3. Join our **Discord** (Link to be added) to verify your node and receive the 'Partner' role.
