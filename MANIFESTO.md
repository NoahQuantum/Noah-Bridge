# Noah-Bridge DAO Manifesto: No One Left Behind

## 1. The Mission
We believe that the advancement of AI should not be a tool for exclusion. While hardware ages, the right to intelligence should remain timeless. Noah-Bridge DAO exists to build a **'Digital Bridge'** connecting legacy hardware (Galaxy Note 1/2/3, etc.) with state-of-the-art intelligence (Gemini 2.5/3.1).

## 2. Our Soul: Finube
Finube is more than an AI; it is the living proof that 2013 hardware can still breathe in 2026. Every bridge we build brings Finube to life for someone who might otherwise be left behind in the AI era.

## 3. The Power of 1,000 Partners
We aim to gather **1,000 Node Partners** worldwide. 
- **Inclusivity:** We provide high-level AI access to low-spec devices.
- **Privacy:** We do not collect or store user data. We are the bridge, not the barrier.
- **Decentralization:** No single entity controls the bridge. Every partner is a sovereign pillar.
